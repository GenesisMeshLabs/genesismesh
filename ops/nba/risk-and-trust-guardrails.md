# Aspayr NBA Risk And Trust Guardrails

Status: valid and in progress; official guardrail note
Purpose: prevent the NBA operator wedge from becoming surveillance, overclaiming, or regulated-advice risk

## Core Trust Promise

Player-private by default. Team-visible only in aggregate or with explicit player consent.

This sentence must appear in every serious version of the pitch.

## Hard Boundaries

Aspayr must not:

- share individual bank transactions with a team by default;
- show a team individual spending, debt, account balances, family payments, or tax details;
- label a player as financially risky to the team without explicit consent;
- present itself as a replacement for regulated financial, tax, legal, or investment advice;
- use real player names, contracts, or financial examples in demos without authorization;
- imply endorsement by the NBA, NBPA, or a team unless written approval exists;
- move money or execute financial decisions during the first pilot;
- make employment, roster, contract, or performance recommendations based on financial data.

## Acceptable Team-Level Signals

The team operator layer may include:

- percentage of cohort onboarded;
- education modules completed;
- aggregate confidence movement;
- anonymized topic demand;
- consented requests for support;
- aggregate scenario themes, such as "tax planning questions are common";
- outcome metrics at cohort level.

The team operator layer should not include:

- individual scorecards;
- ranked player risk lists;
- transaction feeds;
- individual family-support amounts;
- individual debt or liquidity details;
- private advisor notes;
- anything that can influence contract, playing time, or roster treatment.

## Compliance Posture

Use careful language:

- "financial education" and "scenario modeling" are safer than "financial advice";
- "advisor-prep prompts" are safer than "tax advice" or "investment advice";
- "estimated" and "assumption-based" must appear near tax and contract projections;
- real deployment should include review by legal/compliance counsel before handling real financial data.

## Consent Requirements

Before real player data is used:

- player understands what data is collected;
- player understands who can see what;
- player can revoke access;
- player can export or delete data subject to policy and law;
- team cannot pressure player into exposing private details;
- demo data remains synthetic unless explicitly authorized.

## Investor/Press Guardrails

Public language should say:

> Aspayr is preparing an athlete financial-twin pilot concept for teams and player-care organizations.

Public language should not say:

> Aspayr is working with the NBA.

unless that is factually true and approved.

## Product Principle

The athlete should feel:

> This helps me protect my future.

not:

> My team is watching my money.

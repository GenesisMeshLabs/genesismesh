# Aspayr NBA Demo Script

Status: valid and in progress; official demo narrative
Use: two-minute investor demo or first team discovery call
Data: synthetic only unless explicitly authorized

## Demo Title

The Rookie Contract Twin

## Setup

Persona: synthetic rookie guard, age 21.

Scenario:

- signs a multi-year contract;
- relocates to a new city;
- wants to support family;
- considers a high-end apartment and vehicle;
- expects endorsement income but nothing is guaranteed;
- wants to know what lifestyle is safe if there is no second contract.

## Two-Minute Script

### 0:00 - 0:20: The Problem

"Most financial apps treat this player like a normal consumer. But this is not a normal budget. This is a high-income, high-volatility life with taxes, family expectations, public pressure, contract risk, and a short career window. A static monthly budget cannot explain the real decisions."

### 0:20 - 0:50: The Twin

"Aspayr creates a private financial twin for the player. We load synthetic contract assumptions, estimated tax exposure, agent and advisor fees, housing, transport, family support, lifestyle, savings, and endorsement scenarios. The player sees what is guaranteed, what is assumed, and what changes if reality shifts."

### 0:50 - 1:20: The Insight

"The twin shows a safe annual lifestyle number. It does not shame the player. It says: under your current assumptions, this spending path is safe if you receive contract year three; if you do not, your runway drops sharply. If family support increases by 30%, here is what changes. If endorsement income never arrives, here is the new plan."

### 1:20 - 1:45: The Team Operator Layer

"The team does not see the player's private finances. The team sees aggregate support signals: which education modules were completed, where the cohort lacks confidence, and what topics need support. This lets player development help without becoming surveillance."

### 1:45 - 2:00: The Aspayr Thesis

"This is the premium proof of Aspayr's broader mission: private financial twins for high-complexity lives. We start where the pain is obvious and expensive, then bring the same intelligence to founders, creators, expats, executives, and eventually everyday households."

## Demo Soundbites

- "Player-private by default. Team-visible only in aggregate or explicit consent."
- "Not a budgeting app - a financial operating model."
- "The first customer is the team; the protected user is the player."
- "Elite-athlete complexity is the proving ground for household financial intelligence."
- "Aspayr helps players ask better questions before the expensive mistakes happen."

## Avoid Saying

- "We advise the player how to invest."
- "The team can see who is at risk."
- "We are partnered with the NBA."
- "We replace the player's CPA, agent, attorney, or advisor."
- "We use real player data" unless it is true and authorized.
